<p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.5em 0px;"><b style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">这是一个实现者为实施者提供的开源的、可互操作的JavaScript Promise规范</b></p>
<p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.5em 0px;"><code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(233, 105, 0); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">promise</code>代表一个异步操作的最终结果。与<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(233, 105, 0); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">promise</code>的主要互动方式是通过<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(233, 105, 0); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">then</code>方法注册回调函数来接收<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(233, 105, 0); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">promise</code>的最终值或者<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(233, 105, 0); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">promise</code>未完成的原因。</p>
<p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.5em 0px;">该规范详细描述了<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(233, 105, 0); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">then</code>方法的行为，它提供了一个可互操作的基础，所有<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(233, 105, 0); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">Promises/A
+</code> 符合 <code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(233, 105, 0); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">promise</code>的实现都可以依赖于这个基础。因此，该规范被认为是非常稳定的。尽管<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(233, 105, 0); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">Promises/A+</code>的作者可能偶尔会修改规范，对其进行一些向后兼容的小修改，以解决新发现的问题，我们只有在仔细考虑、讨论和测试后，才会集成大型或者不向后兼容的变更。</p>
<p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.5em 0px;">历史上，<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(233, 105, 0); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">Promises/A+</code>澄清了早期的<a href="http://wiki.commonjs.org/wiki/Promises/A" style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; text-decoration: none; color: rgb(30, 107, 184); overflow-wrap: break-word;">Promises/A proposal</a>的行为条款，将其拓展到涵盖事实的行为，并且省略了未指定或有问题的部分。</p>
<p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.5em 0px;">最后，<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(233, 105, 0); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">Promises/A+</code>规范的核心不是处理如何创建、完成或者失败的<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(233, 105, 0); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">promises</code>，而是选择专注于一个可互操作的<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(233, 105, 0); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">then</code>方法。未来在配套规范中的工作可能会涉及这些主题。</p>

<h3 id="h31" style="line-height: inherit; margin: 1.5em 0px; font-weight: bold; font-size: 1.3em; margin-bottom: 2em; margin-right: 5px; padding: 8px 15px; letter-spacing: 2px; background-image: linear-gradient(to right bottom, rgb(0, 188, 212), rgb(63, 81, 181)); background-color: rgb(63, 81, 181); color: rgb(255, 255, 255); border-left: 10px solid rgb(51, 51, 51); border-radius: 5px; text-shadow: rgb(102, 102, 102) 1px 1px 1px; box-shadow: rgb(102, 102, 102) 1px 1px 2px;"><span style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">3.1</span></h3>
<p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.5em 0px;">这里的“平台代码”是指引擎，环境和<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; <h3 id="h33" style="line-height: inherit; margin: 1.5em 0px; font-weight: bold; font-size: 1.3em; margin-bottom: 2em; margin-right: 5px; padding: 8px 15px; letter-spacing: 2px; background-image: linear-gradient(to right bottom, rgb(0, 188, 212), rgb(63, 81, 181)); background-color: rgb(63, 81, 181); color: rgb(255, 255, 255); border-left: 10px solid rgb(51, 51, 51); border-radius: 5px; text-shadow: rgb(102, 102, 102) 1px 1px 1px; box-shadow: rgb(102, 102, 102) 1px 1px 2px;"><span style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">3.3</span></h3>

<h3 id="h34" style="line-height: inherit; margin: 1.5em 0px; font-weight: bold; font-size: 1.3em; margin-bottom: 2em; margin-right: 5px; padding: 8px 15px; letter-spacing: 2px; background-image: linear-gradient(to right bottom, rgb(0, 188, 212), rgb(63, 81, 181)); background-color: rgb(63, 81, 181); color: rgb(255, 255, 255); border-left: 10px solid rgb(51, 51, 51); border-radius: 5px; text-shadow: rgb(102, 102, 102) 1px 1px 1px; box-shadow: rgb(102, 102, 102) 1px 1px 2px;"><span style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">3.4</span></h3>
<p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.5em 0px;">通常，只有<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(233, 105, 0); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">x</code>来自于当前实例时，才知道它是一个真正的<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(233, 105, 0); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">promise</code>，此条款允许使用特定的实现方法来采用已知符合<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(233, 105, 0); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">promise</code>的状态。</p>
<h3 id="h35" style="line-height: inherit; margin: 1.5em 0px; font-weight: bold; font-size: 1.3em; margin-bottom: 2em; margin-right: 5px; padding: 8px 15px; letter-spacing: 2px; background-image: linear-gradient(to right bottom, rgb(0, 188, 212), rgb(63, 81, 181)); background-color: rgb(63, 81, 181); color: rgb(255, 255, 255); border-left: 10px solid rgb(51, 51, 51); border-radius: 5px; text-shadow: rgb(102, 102, 102) 1px 1px 1px; box-shadow: rgb(102, 102, 102) 1px 1px 2px;"><span style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">3.5</span></h3>
<p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.5em 0px;">这个过程首先存储对<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(233, 105, 0); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">x.then</code>的引用，然后测试，然后调用这个引用。避免了对<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(233, 105, 0); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">x.then</code>属性的多次访问。这些预防措施对于确保访问者属性的一致性非常重要，因为访问者属性的值可能在两次检索之间发生变化。</p>
<h3 id="h36" style="line-height: inherit; margin: 1.5em 0px; font-weight: bold; font-size: 1.3em; margin-bottom: 2em; margin-right: 5px; padding: 8px 15px; letter-spacing: 2px; background-image: linear-gradient(to right bottom, rgb(0, 188, 212), rgb(63, 81, 181)); background-color: rgb(63, 81, 181); color: rgb(255, 255, 255); border-left: 10px solid rgb(51, 51, 51); border-radius: 5px; text-shadow: rgb(102, 102, 102) 1px 1px 1px; box-shadow: rgb(102, 102, 102) 1px 1px 2px;"><span style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">3.6</span></h3>
<p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.5em 0px;">实现不应该对<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(233, 105, 0); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">thenable</code>链的深度设置任何限制，并假设递归超出限制。只有真正的循环才会导致<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(233, 105, 0); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">TypeError</code>，如果遇到无穷多个<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(233, 105, 0); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">thenable</code>链，那么永远递归是正确的行为。</p>
