<h3 id="hdiff" style="color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.6em 0px; font-weight: bold; border-bottom: 2px solid rgb(239, 112, 96); font-size: 1.3em;"><span style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; display: inline-block; font-weight: normal; background: rgb(239, 112, 96); color: rgb(255, 255, 255); padding: 3px 10px 1px; border-top-right-radius: 3px; border-top-left-radius: 3px; margin-right: 3px;">diff算法又是什么？</span><span style="display: inline-block; vertical-align: bottom; border-bottom: 36px solid rgb(239, 235, 233); border-right: 20px solid transparent;"> </span></h3>
<p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.7em 0px;"><code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">diff</code>算法，会对比新老虚拟<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">DOM</code>，记录下他们之间的变化，然后将变化的部分更新到视图上。其实之前的<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">diff</code>算法，是通过循环递归每个节点，然后进行对比，复杂程度为<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">O(n^3)</code>，<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">n</code>是树中节点的总数，这样性能是非常差的。</p>
<h3 id="hdomdiff-1" style="color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.6em 0px; font-weight: bold; border-bottom: 2px solid rgb(239, 112, 96); font-size: 1.3em;"><span style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; display: inline-block; font-weight: normal; background: rgb(239, 112, 96); color: rgb(255, 255, 255); padding: 3px 10px 1px; border-top-right-radius: 3px; border-top-left-radius: 3px; margin-right: 3px;">dom-diff的原理</span><span style="display: inline-block; vertical-align: bottom; border-bottom: 36px solid rgb(239, 235, 233); border-right: 20px solid transparent;"> </span></h3>
<p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.7em 0px;"><code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">diff</code>算法会比较前后虚拟<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">DOM</code>，从而得到<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">patches</code>(补丁)，然后与老<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">Virtual DOM</code>进行对比，将其应用在需要更新的地方，得到新的<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">Virtual DOM</code>，在网上有一张非常直观的图可以帮忙参考</p>
<figure style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><img src="https://user-gold-cdn.xitu.io/2020/5/2/171d3ca4cc6af69a?w=1524&amp;h=743&amp;f=png&amp;s=266539" alt="" title="" style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; display: block; margin: 0px auto; max-width: 100%;"><figcaption style="line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; margin-top: 10px; text-align: center; color: rgb(153, 153, 153); font-size: 0.7em;"></figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.7em 0px;">来解释下这张图：现有一个真实的<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">DOM</code>，首先会映射为虚拟<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">DOM</code>，这个时候，我们删除了最后一个<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">p</code>节点和<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">son2</code>的节点，得到了新的一个虚拟<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">DOM</code>，新的<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">vdom</code>会和旧的<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">vdom</code>进行差异对比，得到了<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">pathes</code>对象，之后，对旧的真实<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">dom</code>进行操作，得到了新的<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">DOM</code>。</p>
<h3 id="hdiff-1" style="color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.6em 0px; font-weight: bold; border-bottom: 2px solid rgb(239, 112, 96); font-size: 1.3em;"><span style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; display: inline-block; font-weight: normal; background: rgb(239, 112, 96); color: rgb(255, 255, 255); padding: 3px 10px 1px; border-top-right-radius: 3px; border-top-left-radius: 3px; margin-right: 3px;">diff的几种策略</span><span style="display: inline-block; vertical-align: bottom; border-bottom: 36px solid rgb(239, 235, 233); border-right: 20px solid transparent;"> </span></h3>
<ul style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; padding-left: 32px; list-style-type: disc;">
<li style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; margin-bottom: 0.5em;"><p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.7em 0px;"><code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">Web UI</code>中<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">DOM</code>节点跨层级的移动操作特别少，可以忽略不计。</p></li>
<li style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; margin-bottom: 0.5em;"><p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.7em 0px;">拥有相同类的两个组件将会生成相似的树形结构，拥有不同类的两个组件将会生成不同的树形结构。</p></li>
<li style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; margin-bottom: 0.5em;"><p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.7em 0px;">对于同一层级的一组子节点，它们可以通过唯一<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">id</code>进行区分。</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.7em 0px;">基于以上三个策略，<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">React</code>分别对<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">tree diff</code>、<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">component diff</code>以及<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">element diff</code>进行了算法优化。</p>
<h4 id="htreediff" style="color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.6em 0px; font-weight: bold; font-size: 1.2em;"><span style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">tree diff</span></h4>
<p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.7em 0px;">基于第一个策略，<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">react</code>只会对同一层次的节点进行比较，如下图中，只对颜色相同框内的<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">DOM</code>节点进行比较，当发现节点不存在时，就会删除整个节点及其子节点，不会再进行比较，这样就只需要遍历一次，就能完成对整个<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">DOM</code>树的比较</p>
<figure style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><img src="https://user-gold-cdn.xitu.io/2020/5/18/17226b51df6d86db?w=1800&amp;h=782&amp;f=jpeg&amp;s=140736" alt="" title="" style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; display: block; margin: 0px auto; max-width: 100%;"><figcaption style="line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; margin-top: 10px; text-align: center; color: rgb(153, 153, 153); font-size: 0.7em;"></figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.7em 0px;">如果出现了<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">DOM</code>节点的跨层级的移动操作，<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">React diff</code>会怎样呢？</p>
<p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.7em 0px;"><code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">React</code>只会简单的考虑同层级节点的位置变换，对于不同层级的节点，只有创建和删除操作。如果A节点整个被移动到D节点下，根节点发现子节点中A不见了，就会销毁A；然后D 发现自己多了一个子节点，就会创建新的子节点（包含其中属于自己的子节点）作为其子节点。<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">react diff</code>就会按照这样的次序执行：<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">craete a -&gt; create b -&gt; create c -&gt; delete a</code>。这种跨层级的节点移动，并不会出现移动的情况，而是会有创建、删除这些操作。这种操作会影响到React的性能，所以React官方也并不建议进行这种操作。在开发组件时，保持稳定的dom结构会有助于性能的提升</p>
<figure style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><img src="https://user-gold-cdn.xitu.io/2020/5/18/17226aaa64af0fc8?w=1800&amp;h=782&amp;f=jpeg&amp;s=91609" alt="" title="" style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; display: block; margin: 0px auto; max-width: 100%;"><figcaption style="line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; margin-top: 10px; text-align: center; color: rgb(153, 153, 153); font-size: 0.7em;"></figcaption></figure>
<h4 id="hcomponentdiff" style="color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.6em 0px; font-weight: bold; font-size: 1.2em;"><span style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">component diff</span></h4>
<p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.7em 0px;"><code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">React</code>对于组件间的比较采取的策略也是简洁高效</p>
<ul style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; padding-left: 32px; list-style-type: disc;">
<li style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; margin-bottom: 0.5em;"><p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.7em 0px;">如果是同一类型的组件，按照原策略继续比较虚拟dom树</p></li>
<li style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; margin-bottom: 0.5em;"><p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.7em 0px;">如果不是，则将该组件判断为<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">dirty component</code>，从而替换整个组件下的所有子节点</p></li>
<li style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; margin-bottom: 0.5em;"><p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.7em 0px;">对于同一类型的组件，有可能其<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">Virtual DOM</code>没有任何变化，如果能够确切的知道这点那可以节省大量的<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">diff</code>运算的时间，因此<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">React</code>允许用户通过<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">shouldComponentUpdate()</code>判断该组件是否需要进行<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">diff</code></p></li>
</ul>
<p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.7em 0px;">举个例子来说，当下图中<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">componentD</code>改变为<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">componentG</code>时，即使这两个<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">compoent</code>结构很相似，但是<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">react</code>会判断D和G并不是同类型组件，也就不会比较二者的结构了，而是直接删除了d，重新创建G及其子节点，这个时候会影响react的性能</p>
<figure style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><img src="https://user-gold-cdn.xitu.io/2020/5/18/17226a8076dfb633?w=1800&amp;h=782&amp;f=jpeg&amp;s=103490" alt="" title="" style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; display: block; margin: 0px auto; max-width: 100%;"><figcaption style="line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; margin-top: 10px; text-align: center; color: rgb(153, 153, 153); font-size: 0.7em;"></figcaption></figure>
<h4 id="helementdiff" style="color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.6em 0px; font-weight: bold; font-size: 1.2em;"><span style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">element diff</span></h4>
<p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.7em 0px;">当节点处于同一层级时，<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">React diff</code>提供了三种节点操作：插入、移动和删除</p>
<ul style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; padding-left: 32px; list-style-type: disc;">
<li style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; margin-bottom: 0.5em;"><p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.7em 0px;">插入：新的<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">component</code>类型不在老集合里 -&gt; 全新的节点，需要对新节点执行插入操作</p></li>
<li style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; margin-bottom: 0.5em;"><p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.7em 0px;">移动：在老集合里有新<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">component</code>类型，且<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">element</code>是可更新的类型，<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">generateComponentChildren</code>已调用<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">receiveComponent</code>，这种情况下<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">prevChild=nextChild</code>，就需要做移动操作，可以复用以前的<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">dom</code>节点</p></li>
<li style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; margin-bottom: 0.5em;"><p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.7em 0px;">删除：老的<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">component</code>类型，在新集合中也有，但对应的<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">element</code>不同则不能直接复用和更新，需要执行删除操作，或者老<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">component</code>不在新集合里，也需要执行删除操作</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.7em 0px;">举个🌰：看下图中，老集合中包含节点A、B、C、D，更新后的集合中包含节点B、A、D、C，此时进行新老集合差异化对比，发现B不等于A，则创建并插入了B至新集合，删除老集合A，以此类推。。。这样做很繁琐，因为这些都是相同的节点，只是位置发生了变化，针对这一现象，react提出了优化策略，允许开发者对同一层级的同组子节点，添加唯一key进行区分【<strong style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-weight: bold; color: rgb(233, 105, 0);">注意：这里就体现了key的作用～</strong>】</p>
<figure style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><img src="https://user-gold-cdn.xitu.io/2020/5/18/1722697a8a13fc6e?w=1000&amp;h=500&amp;f=jpeg&amp;s=58536" alt="" title="" style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; display: block; margin: 0px auto; max-width: 100%;"><figcaption style="line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; margin-top: 10px; text-align: center; color: rgb(153, 153, 153); font-size: 0.7em;"></figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.7em 0px;">上面我们叙述的情况是没<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">key</code>的情况，如果有<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">key</code>了(假设<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">key</code>为上图中每个节点对应的名字，例如节点A，对应<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">key</code>为A)，它就会这么对比：</p>
<p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.7em 0px;"><code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">diff</code>会通过<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">key</code>发现新老集合中的节点是相同的节点，因此无需进行节点删除和创建，只需要将老集合中节点的位置进行移动，更新为新集合中节点的位置，此时<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">React</code>给出的<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">diff</code>结果为：B、D不做任何操作，A、C进行移动操作。</p>
<p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.7em 0px;">首先对新集合的节点进行循环遍历，通过唯一<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">key</code>可以判断新老集合中是否存在相同的节点，如果存在，则进行移动操作，但在移动前需要将当前节点在老集合中的位置与<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">lastIndex</code>进行比较，如果<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">节点当前的位置&lt;lastIndex</code>，则进行节点移动操作，否则不执行该操作。这是一种顺序优化手段，<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">lastIndex</code>一直在更新，表示访问过的节点在老集合中最右的位置（即最大的位置），如果新集合中当前访问的节点比<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">lastIndex</code>大，说明当前访问节点在老集合中就比上一个节点位置靠后，则该节点不会影响其他节点的位置，因此不用添加到差异队列中，即不执行移动操作，只有当访问的节点比<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">lastIndex</code>小时，才需要进行移动操作</p>
<p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.7em 0px;">下面我们来说一下大致的过程（下面所说的<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">_mountIndex</code>是当前节点所在位置，<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">lastIndex</code>为参考位置）</p>
<p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.7em 0px;">1、从新集合中取到B，判断老集合中存在相同的节点，通过对比节点位置判读是否进行了移动操作，B在老集合中的位置为<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">_mountIndex=1</code>，此时<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">lastIndex = 0</code>，不满足<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">child._mountIndex &lt; lastIndex</code>的条件，因此不对B进行移动操作；此时更新<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">lastIndex = Math.max(prevChild._mountIndex, lastIndex)</code>，[其中prevChild.mountIndex表示B在老集合中的位置]。<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">lastIndex=1</code>，并将B的位置更新为新集合中的位置<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">prevChild._mountIndex=nextIndex</code>，此时新集合中<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">B._mountIndex=0</code>，<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">nextIndex++</code>进入下一个节点的判断</p>
<p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.7em 0px;">2、从新集合中取得A，判断老集合中存在相同节点A，通过对比节点位置判断是否进行进行移动操作，A在老集合中的位置<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">A._mountIndex=0</code>，此时<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">lastIndex=1</code>，满足<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">child.mountIndex&lt;lastIndex</code>的条件，因此对A进行移动操作<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">enqueueMove(this, child._mountIndex, toIndex)</code>，其中 <code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">toIndex</code> 其实就是 <code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">nextIndex</code>，表示 A 需要移动到的位置；更新 <code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">lastIndex = Math.max(prevChild._mountIndex, lastIndex)</code>，则 <code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">lastIndex ＝ 1</code>，并将 <code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">A</code> 的位置更新为新集合中的位置 <code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">prevChild._mountIndex = nextIndex</code>，此时新集合中<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">A._mountIndex = 1，nextIndex++</code> 进入下一个节点的判断。</p>
<p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.7em 0px;">3、从新集合中取到D，判断老集合中是否存在相同节点，通过对比位置判断是否进行移动操作，D在老集合中的位置<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">D._mountIndex=3</code>，此时<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">lastIndex=1</code>，不满足<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">child._mountIndex&lt;lastIndex</code>的条件，因此不对D进行移动操作；更新<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">lastIndex=Math.max(prevChild._mountIndex, lastIndex)</code>，则<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">lastIndex=3</code>，并将D的位置更新为新集合中的位置<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">prevChild._mountIndex=nextIndex</code>，此时新集合中<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">D._mountIndex=2</code>，<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">nextIndex++</code>进入下一个节点的判断</p>
<p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.7em 0px;">4、C节点同理</p>
<p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.7em 0px;"><strong style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-weight: bold; color: rgb(233, 105, 0);">如果新老集合中，不只是存在位置互换的关系呢？React diff又如何进行操作？（下图中各节点的key为对应节点名称）</strong></p>
<figure style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><img src="https://user-gold-cdn.xitu.io/2020/5/18/17226b75575ac858?w=1000&amp;h=500&amp;f=jpeg&amp;s=58359" alt="" title="" style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; display: block; margin: 0px auto; max-width: 100%;"><figcaption style="line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; margin-top: 10px; text-align: center; color: rgb(153, 153, 153); font-size: 0.7em;"></figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.7em 0px;">1、从新集合中取到B，判断老集合中是否存在相同的节点，找到B在老集合中的位置<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">B._mountIndex=1</code>，此时<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">lastIndex=0</code>，因此不对B进行移动操作；更新<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">lastIndex=1</code>，并将B的位置更新为新集合中的位置<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">B._mountIndex=0</code>，nextIndex++进入下一个节点的判断</p>
<p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.7em 0px;">2、取到新集合中E节点，由于老集合中不存在相同的节点，则创建新节点E；更新<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">lastIndex=1</code>，并将E的位置更新为新集合中的位置，<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">nextIndex++</code>进入下个节点的判断。</p>
<p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.7em 0px;">3、取到新集合中C节点，老集合中存在相同节点，<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">C._mountIndex=2</code>，<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">lastIndex=1</code>，此时<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">C._mountIndex &gt; lastIndex</code>，因此不对C进行移动操作；更新<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">lastIndex=2</code>，并将C的位置更新为新集合中的位置，nextIndex++进入下一个节点的判断。</p>
<p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.7em 0px;">4、取到新集合中A节点，老集合中存在相同的节点，<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">A._mountIndex=0</code>，<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">lastIndex=2</code>，此时<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">A._mountIndex &lt; lastIndex</code>，对A进行移动操作；更新<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">lastIndex=2</code>，并将A的位置更新为新集合中的位置，<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">nextIndex++</code>进入下一个节点的判断。</p>
<p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.7em 0px;">5、当完成新集合中的所有节点的<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">diff</code>时，最后还需要对老集合进行循环遍历，判断是否存在新集合中没有但老集合中仍存在的节点，发现存在这样的节点D，因此删除节点D，到此<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">diff</code>全部完成。</p>
<h2 id="h-1" style="color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.6em 0px; font-weight: bold; font-size: 1.4em;"><span style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">总结</span></h2>
<p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.7em 0px;">基于<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">diff</code>这样的策略，所以<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">react</code>建议我们用添加唯一<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">key</code>的方式来进行优化，这里面可以牵扯出来一个问题：</p>
<p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.7em 0px;"><strong style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-weight: bold; color: rgb(233, 105, 0);">如果用index作为key会有什么问题呢？</strong></p>
<p style="font-size: inherit; color: inherit; line-height: inherit; padding: 0px; margin: 1.7em 0px;"><code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">index</code>作为<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">key</code>，如果我们删除了一个节点，那么数组的后一项可能会前移，这个时候移动的节点和删除的节点就是相同的<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">key</code>了，在<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">react</code>中，如果<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">key</code>相同，就会视为相同的组件，但这两个组件并不是相同的，这就会出现一些我们不想看到的问题～所以<code style="font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; overflow-wrap: break-word; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 0px 2px; color: rgb(248, 35, 117); background: rgb(248, 248, 248);">key</code>的值我们要考虑好再确定哦~</p>
